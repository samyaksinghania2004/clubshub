Help / FAQ

How do I find events?

Use the Events page to browse published events. You can filter by club, date, and tag, or use the search page for keywords.

How do I join open rooms?

Open the Open Rooms page, choose a room, and set a handle. Public open rooms let you join immediately. Private open rooms require an invite.

What are handles and reveal actions?

Your handle is the name other people see inside a room. Room creators or administrators can take moderation action when required. In open rooms, the creator can reveal a handle and expel that participant.

Can I change event registrations?

You can register or cancel while the event is still open and has not yet started. If an event is full, you may be placed on the waitlist.
